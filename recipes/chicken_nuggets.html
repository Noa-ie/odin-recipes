Chicken Nuggets

Perfectly delicious, these hidden veggie chicken nuggets are smuggling three different veggies! Great for getting veggies into the kids!

Ingredients

Filling

  • 500 grams chicken mince
  • 1 egg
  • ½ head caulifower chopped into florets
  • ½ head broccoli
  • 2 carrots grated

Coating

  • 1 egg lightly whisked with ¾ cup water
  • ¾ cup plain flour
  • 1.5 cups panko crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees. (355F)
  2. Combine the mince, egg, cauliflower, broccoli and carrots into a food processor.
  3. Process until smooth and like a dough.
  4. With wet hands, form tablespoonfuls into nugget shapes.
  5. Coat in flour, then dip into the egg water then coat in the panko crumbs.
  6. Space them out on a lined baking tray and drizzle with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes at 180 degrees celsius, then flip and bake for a further 20 minutes. (355F)
